# Lynx

Data from [The Ten-Year Cycle in Numbers of the Lynx in Canada](https://doi.org/10.2307/1358) by
Charles Elton and Mary Nicholson published in _Journal of Animal Ecology_ (Vol.
11, No. 2, November 1942), pp. 215-244.

Data from three major tables are included in the following files:

- `table-1.csv`
- `table-4.csv` and
- `table-5.csv`.

## Table 1

Data dictionary:

- `year`: Year (1821–1912)
- `hbco_2yr`: Hudson's Bay Company (1878) moved back 2 years.
- `poland_1yr`: Poland (1892) moved back 1 year.
- `seton_1yr`: Seton (1912) moved back 1 year.
- `jones_2yr`: Jones (1914) moved back 2 years.
- `hewitt_1yr`: Hewitt (1921) moved back 1 year.
- `hewitt_2yr`: Hewitt (1921) moved back 2 years.
